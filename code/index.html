Effects of heavy weight lifting

Heavy weight lifting has been proven to be the most efficiant way to gain muscles and reduce fat mass.

Muscle burns more calories at rest than fat. More muscle = higher resting metabolic rate. It stimulates bone growth, reducing the risk of osteoporosis as you age. Boosts testosterone, growth hormone, and IGF-1 levels, which are all linked to muscle growth, fat burning, and better recovery.
